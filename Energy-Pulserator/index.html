Rules of the energy field

  1. The energy field is composed of a grid. And each point in the grid is called a "Pulser".
  2. At the start. Each Pulser has a random energy level assigned from 0 to 9.
  3. If a Pulser has 0 energy it is black.
  4. If a Pulser has less than 5 energy it is green.
  5. If a Pulser has more than 5 energy, but less than 10 energy it is yellow.
  6. If a Pulser has more than 10 energy it is red.
  7. When a Pulser has more than 10 energy it will create a pulse every second which
    increases the energy of every Pulser directly around it by 1 and decreases its own energy by the energy loss per pulse factor.
    It also turns white momentarily.
  8. Pressing the "PULSE" button increases the energy of every Pulser in the grid by 1.
  9. Pressing the "LOCAL PULSE" button increases the energy of a random pulsr in the field by 1.
  10. Pressing the "RESET" button resets the energy field to point 2.
